What

We are a Cali-based Japanese streetwear brand with inspiration from traditional Japanese aesthetics mixed with a contemporary Bay Area/LA attitude. We’re here to give you streetwear that’s truly one of a kind. One to be jealous of.

Tradition

We find our identity in Japanese folklore and the story of the Hannya—a beautiful women twisted into a kijo (demoness) from betrayal or Jealousy. It is from this story we derive and infuse the dangerous & artistic beauty found in our designs. Displaying the hannya mask, the mask of the kijo often used in traditional Noh plays, is rumored to have the ability to ward off evil but also ties into the buddhist concept of true wisdom.

An aesthetic of betrayal, beauty, and jealousy.

For more on the Hannya check out the traditional works of: Aoi no Ue, Kurozuka, Dojo-ji.
